Gem Vault

    There will be four gems stones displayed on this page

  • You will be given a randomly generated target score at the start of the game.
  • Clicking on the gems will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
    • The game will hide the value of these points until you click a crystal.
    • When one is clicked, it updates your score.
  • To win your score must be the same as the target number displayed.
  • You loses if your score goes above the random number.
  • The game restarts whenever the player wins or loses, click the reset button to restart.
